Quick answers.

What does Staitment actually do?
+

She's an AI personal assistant — but instead of one general chatbot, you get four expert agents. Style picks your outfits and analyzes your color season. Travel plans your trips with real flights and hotels. Fitness finds classes near you. Lifestyle handles reminders, gift ideas, and the small stuff. All in one chat.

Is it free?
+

Free to download. Free to chat with any agent up to a generous monthly limit. A small subscription unlocks unlimited conversations, photo analyses, and saved calendars. Cancel anytime.

How is this different from ChatGPT?
+

ChatGPT gives you the same answer it gives everyone. Staitment knows you — your style, your city, your schedule. And she's built specifically for the things you actually deal with: outfits, trips, classes, life. Plus she returns rich answers (real flight options, real color palettes, real class bookings), not walls of text.

How private is private?
+

Conversations, photos, and saved items are encrypted at rest. We never sell data. We never use your chats to train models. Export or delete everything in one tap.

Who's it for?
+

Women who are busy, ambitious, and juggling a lot. Marketing managers, freelancers, grad students, new moms. If you already use AI casually but want something that knows *you* and handles the stuff your other apps can't — you're the one.
