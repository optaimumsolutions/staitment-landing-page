Common questions

Quick answers.

What does Staitment actually do?
+

It's an AI personal assistant with four expert agents. Style analyzes your color season, rates outfits, and manages your digital wardrobe. Travel searches real flights and hotels via Amadeus with instant booking links. Fitness finds studios near you with ClassPass and Mindbody links, and creates BPM-matched playlists on your Spotify. Lifestyle syncs your Google Calendar, sets reminders, and remembers your preferences across every conversation.

Is it free?
+

Free to download. Free to chat with any agent up to a generous monthly limit. A small subscription unlocks unlimited conversations, photo analyses, and connected services. Cancel anytime.

How is this different from ChatGPT?
+

ChatGPT gives you the same answer it gives everyone. Staitment learns you — your color season, your travel budget, your fitness level, your schedule. And it's connected to real services: Spotify for playlists, Amadeus for flights, Google Places for restaurants, your calendar for availability. Four specialized agents with strict expertise means better answers, not generic ones.

What does Staitment connect to?
+

Spotify for playlists, music search, and now playing. Google Calendar for schedule awareness and conflict detection. Amadeus for live flight and hotel pricing. Google Places for real restaurants, gyms, and attractions. ClassPass and Mindbody for studio booking links. All connections use secure OAuth. Disconnect anytime.

How private is private?
+

Conversations, photos, and saved items are encrypted at rest. We never sell data. We never use your chats to train models. Connected services use standard OAuth — we never see your passwords. Delete your account and all data anytime.

What does the knowledge graph do?
+

Staitment automatically learns your preferences from conversations — your style, budget, dietary needs, travel habits, fitness goals. These facts are shared across all four agents so you never repeat yourself. View, edit, or delete any fact from your "About You" screen at any time.

Who is Staitment for?
+

Anyone who's busy and wants an AI that actually does things instead of just talking about them. If you use Spotify, manage a calendar, care about your style, and want one app that ties it all together — Staitment is built for you.
