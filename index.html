Okay but what does she actually do?
+

Four things, really well. Style: color analysis from a selfie, honest outfit ratings, digital wardrobe from your photos. Fitness: real studios near you via ClassPass and Mindbody + Spotify workout playlists matched to your BPM. Lifestyle: calendar sync, reminders that fire, and a memory that holds it all.

Is this free, or is there a catch?
+

Free to download. Free to chat — generously — every month. If you fall in love and want unlimited conversations, photo analyses, and connected services, there's a small subscription. Cancel any time, no shame, no dark patterns.

Why not just use ChatGPT?
+

You can, and the answer will be a paragraph. Staitment gives you the actual flight at $412, the actual studio 0.4 miles away with class at 6pm Tuesday, the actual playlist on your Spotify. She also knows you — your color season, your budget, that you hate stopovers. ChatGPT introduces itself to a stranger every time. Staitment is the friend who remembers.

What does she connect to?
+

Spotify (playlists, search, your top tracks). Google Calendar (your real schedule). Amadeus (live flight and hotel pricing). Google Places (restaurants, studios, the good ones). ClassPass and Mindbody for booking. All secure OAuth — she never sees your passwords. Unplug anything, anytime.

How private is this, actually?
+

Real talk: your conversations, photos, and saved items are encrypted at rest. We don't sell your data — ever. We don't train models on your chats. Connected services use standard OAuth, so we never see your passwords. Want it all gone? Delete your account and every fact about you with it — one tap.

What's the memory thing — does she just listen to everything?
+

She pulls out the useful facts: your color season, your budget range, dietary stuff, your travel style, your fitness goals. Those facts are shared across all four agents so you stop introducing yourself every conversation. You can see every fact she's holding on your "About You" screen, edit it, or delete any of it. You're always in charge of your file.

Is this for me?
+

If you're the friend who plans every trip, books every dinner, and runs your life like a one-woman ops department — yes. If you use Spotify, you live in your calendar, you care what you wear, and you want one app that finally connects the dots you've been holding in your head — Staitment is built for you.
